Services

We offer a full set of services to help you launch, test, and optimize healthcare bots.

Design and Configure

Our user experience and conversational design teams craft intuitive and tailored patient experiences to improve outcomes across your organization.

Integrate

Patient engagement succeeds when it’s personal. Our integration teams build the two-way connections with your internal EMR systems so that patient-specific data will inform and shape every patient conversation at scale, and the “real world” data collected in those conversations can make it back into the EMR systems.

Support

We support your bot deployments with robust training, troubleshooting, and premium service level guarantees.

Manage

Need hands-on help? Work directly with a LifeLink success manager to monitor, optimize, and tune your patient conversations. LifeLink also works with leading industry leading care management services companies. Learn about the LifeLink CareThrough partnership on our News page.
